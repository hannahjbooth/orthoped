OrthoPed splint future availability

The children's OrthoPed splint will be launched in 2025, with the adult's OrthoPed splint being launched in 2026.

How to decide which OrthoPed splint size to order

To measure the size of OrthoPed splint use a tape measure around the widest part of your ankle as shown in the picture below.
